Who We Are

We are a globally certified and recognised reseller of premium German automotive brands including BMW, Audi, Mercedes-Benz and Porsche. Our dealership is committed to delivering excellence, performance and luxury through every vehicle we offer.

Behind our doors is a dedicated team focused on customer support and satisfaction. From the front desk to our service department, every member of our team is committed to providing a seamless and professional experience for our clients. As one of the leading automotive dealers in Botswana, we take pride in being a top seller of German performance and luxury vehicles. We continue to grow our reputation by offering quality, reliability and trusted service. We also offer a range of customisation options to meet individual preferences, ensuring that every customer finds a vehicle that suits their needs, lifestyle and personal standards.
