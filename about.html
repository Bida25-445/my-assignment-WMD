About Schwarzwald Autos

Driven by performance, luxury and German engineering excellence.

Who We Are

We are a globally certified and recognised reseller of premium German automotive brands including BMW, Audi, Mercedes-Benz and Porsche.

Our dealership is committed to delivering excellence, performance and luxury through every vehicle we offer.

Behind our doors is a dedicated team focused on customer support and satisfaction, from the front desk to the service department.

As one of the leading automotive dealers in Botswana, we take pride in offering quality, reliability and trusted service.

We also offer a range of customisation options to meet individual preferences, ensuring that every customer finds a vehicle that suits their needs, lifestyle and personal standards.

Our Mission

To provide premium vehicles and a trusted customer experience.

Our Vision

To become a leading destination for German automotive excellence.

Our Values

Quality, integrity, performance and customer satisfaction.
